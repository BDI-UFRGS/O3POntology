Object Property: hasRole

Annotations (7)

  • rdfs:label "has role" @en-us
  • skos:example "this person has role this investigator role (more colloquially: this person has this role of investigator)" @en-us
  • adaptedFrom "http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/RO_0000087"
  • explanatoryNote "A bearer can have many roles, and its roles can exist for different periods of time, but none of its roles can exist when the bearer does not exist. A role need not be realized at all the times that the role exists." @en-us
  • firstOrderLogicAxiom "hasRole(x,y) → IndependentContinuant(x) ∧ Role(y) ∧ bearerOf(x,y)"
  • naturalLanguageDefinition "relation from an independent continuant (the bearer) to a role, in which the role specifically depends on the bearer for its existence" @en-us
  • semiFormalNaturalLanguageAxiom "x has role y holds when x is a 'independent continuant' and y is a 'role' and x is 'bearer of' y"
